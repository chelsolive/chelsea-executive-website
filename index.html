My Approach

I'm Chelsea Olivia. I survived. I rebuilt. I learned systems. And now I help leaders do the same.

For nearly a decade, I worked in corporate management and leadership roles across multiple industries. I built and led teams, trained new employees, and created systems that actually worked. In those 9 years, I noticed a pattern: most organizations wait until chaos hits to build systems. By then, it's too late. Employees are burning out. Communication is broken. Contractors and clients are confused. And the founder is drowning.

I saw how the right structures—built intentionally and early—could transform everything. How clear communication prevents pipeline disasters. How systems for employee retention, delegation, and accountability actually free up leaders instead of constraining them. That's when I realized: I couldn't just observe this anymore. I had to do it myself.

Today, I serve as Director of Strategic Operations and Systems for BuildingHER Executives, where I apply these principles at scale. And through OA Executive Systems, I work with select founders and CEOs to build the operational infrastructure their businesses deserve—before the problems start.

My journey taught me something most consultants never learn: operational excellence isn't just about efficiency. It's about creating space for clarity, purpose, and authentic leadership.

I don't believe in cookie-cutter frameworks or one-size-fits-all solutions. Every business is different. Every leader is different. That's why I work with a limited number of clients each year—so I can truly understand your vision and design systems that reflect who you are.

Through OA Executive Systems, I combine strategic operational design with identity-centered leadership. Your operations should support your vision, not hold you back. Your systems should reflect your values, not compromise them.
